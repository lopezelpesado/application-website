The ABOUT page has a bit about why I am applying to Founders and Coders.

The MAGIC page has a life counting app for the collectible card game Magic: The Gathering (MTG). In MTG, your life total is an important element of the game and can go up and down from turn to turn. When your life goes to 0, you lose! So it’s vital that you keep track of how much life you and your opponents have left. This app will help you do just that.
